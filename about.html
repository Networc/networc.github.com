About Me

I am currently a Captain in the U.S. Army with a B.S. in Computer Science from the United States Military Academy. I will be attending the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill to obtain a Master's in Computer Science. Afterwards, I will return to West Point as an instructor in the Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Department.

On this site you will find links to my open source projects hosted on GitHub as well as blog posts describing my projects and future research. Please +1 and Tweet about the projects and blog posts that you find useful or interesting.
